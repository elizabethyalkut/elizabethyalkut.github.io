Prophecies, Libels and Dreams, by Ysabeau S. Wilce

Smart, inventive, and unusual, but sadly not to my taste — someone with a lot more interest in American mythmaking would probably get a lot more out of this. Loved the faux-scholarly “afterword” to each story, and the title is so great it hurts my heart.
